Introduction

We understand the importance of getting a project completed on time and hence always work in a planned fashion keeping some tolerance in mind to achieve the same. As a part of our quality policy, we only take a limited number of projects at once, to ensure that each project meets the quality standard as it deserves. Due to the nature of the business, our main client continues to remain Nepal electricity Authority where we have executed 5 Substation Contracts, 72 EPC Contracts, and more than 40 supply projects successfully till date.
